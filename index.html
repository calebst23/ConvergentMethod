Meet Our Team

The Convergence of Excellence

Convergent Method was established with a clear vision to merge distinct practices rarely integrated into a single company. We combine the expertise of contractors, engineers, and healthcare providers to create the unique Convergent Method.


Our team possesses the knowledge and experience necessary to provide unparalleled value to our clients. We strive to bring excellence with a holistic approach to your project, starting from the initial concept through the construction phase and ultimately to long-term sustainable operations.


We aim to reduce maintenance costs over the lifespan of your building while taking care of your team, patients, and customers. In addition to our core services, we prioritize sustainability and environmental responsibility, ensuring that your building not only meets its immediate needs but also contributes to a healthier, greener future.

Meet Our Team

Mark Stemmans, PE

Construction Program Manager

Mark is a founding member of Convergent Method and has been passionate about construction from a young age. A Texas Civil Professional Engineer, he has managed construction projects totaling billions, including research laboratories, hospitals, airports, and complex infrastructure.


He currently oversees the construction of the McNair campus at Baylor St. Luke's Medical Center. Mark values ethical decision-making, budget management, and owner satisfaction.

Amanda Stemmans, MS, APRN, FNP-C

Founding Member

Amanda began her career as a staff nurse in the operating room and earned her Master of Science degree from Texas Woman's University, graduating summa cum laude. She is a licensed Family Nurse Practitioner in Texas and Louisiana.


Her passion for healthcare and serving others is evident in her unwavering commitment to excellence. She values building meaningful relationships, effective communication, and leading a life that upholds dignity and respect for all.
