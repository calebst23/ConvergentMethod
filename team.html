Meet Our Team

Mark Stemmans, PE

Founding Member

Mark is a founding member of Convergent Method and has been passionate about construction from a young age. He loves to create, build, and make things better. Mark is committed to providing each project with the highest quality program management and leadership. He values ethical decision-making, budget and time management, creativity, innovation, teamwork, and owner satisfaction. Mark is the Construction Program Manager at Baylor St. Luke’s Medical Center in Houston, TX. He successfully oversees the construction of their McNair campus, which includes a new ambulatory care center and hospital tower renovation/expansion in the premiere Houston Medical Center. As Construction Program Manager, Mark is responsible for leading the owner-representative team, managing all financial reporting, contract administration, requests for payment, schedule management, and overall project coordination. He is a Texas Civil Professional Engineer and has managed construction projects totaling billions. His experience includes managing large projects in congested areas, renovating occupied facilities, research laboratories, hospitals, mental health facilities, maximum security prisons, airport terminals, class A office buildings, garages, pedestrian bridges, roadway infrastructure, light rail stations, and complex mechanical, data, and electrical systems.

Amanda Stemmans, MS, APRN, FNP-C

Founding Member

Amanda, one of the founding members of Convergent Method, began her career as a staff nurse in the operating room. Through years of dedication and hard work, she has climbed the ranks and earned her Master of Science degree from Texas Woman’s University in Houston, TX, graduating summa cum laude. She is a licensed Family Nurse Practitioner in Texas and Louisiana. Amanda’s passion for healthcare and serving others is evident in her unwavering commitment to excellence. She values building meaningful relationships, effective communication, and leading a life that upholds dignity and respect for all. Her pursuit of knowledge and innovation is remarkable, and she constantly seeks new ways to make a positive impact. Amanda is a true leader, and her confidence in her abilities and her commitment to her values inspire all who work with her.

Who We Are

Our Story

Convergent Method was established with a clear vision to merge distinct practices rarely integrated into a single company. We combine the expertise of contractors, engineers, and healthcare providers to create the unique Convergent Method. Our team possesses the knowledge and experience necessary to provide unparalleled value to our clients. In addition to our core services, we prioritize sustainability and environmental responsibility in all our projects, ensuring that your building not only meets its immediate needs but also contributes to a healthier, greener future. Our holistic approach extends beyond construction, as we are committed to optimizing the operational efficiency of your facility, leading to reduced energy consumption and long-term cost savings. By taking care of your team, patients, and customers, we aim to foster a welcoming and supportive environment that enhances the overall experience for all stakeholders involved.
